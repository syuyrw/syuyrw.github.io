Target Audience

Description of Target Audience

The target audience for this project is devided into two main categories. Locals, and visitors. Local refers to anybody who lives in Utah County, and visitor refers to somebody who lives outside of the county, but will be visiting.

Two Personas

Persona 1:

Name: Jane Doe

Age: 59

Income: $80,000 Anually

Experience with the topic: Enjoys looking at weather forecasts online to help her plan her day

Devices: iPhone, Desktop PC

Persona 2:

Name: John Doe

Age: 64

Income: $75,000 Anually

Experience with the topic: Very little experience at all.

Devices: Android, iPad

Scenarios

Scenario 1:

A college student is planning a camping trip with a bunch of his buddies. He checks Utah County Weather Wiz to see if it is going to be safe to go on the camping trip this weekend, or if he should wait another week.

Scenario 2:

A young boy wants to go and see the baseball game tonight, but he's not sure if he needs to bring a rain coat or not. He checks Utah County Weather Wiz to see what the weather will be.

Scenario 3:

A set of grandparents are going to visit their grandkids out of state. They have heard that it might rain, and want to see what fun things they can do with their grandkids in the rain while they are visiting.

Scenario 4:

An older woman feels aching in he knee. Usually she that tells her that there is a storm coming, but she checks Utah County Weather Wiz to make sure before she brings he pets inside.

Scenario 5:

A young girl and her friends want to go and see a concert tonight, but they don't want to get rained on. She checks Utah County Weather Wiz to see if it is supposed to rain tonight.
